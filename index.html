Why ACS?

The company’s initial intent was to develop simple devices
to replace existing pouches, to support personnel with the use of their weaponry while under stress.
All our Tac Holster products have a variety of advantages, compared to the existing pouches:

  • Made in Israel – proven in the battle field
  • MILStd 810G – ready for military and law-enforcement use
  • Patented – Uniquely developed for the end-user
  • Safety – Several levels of protection – safety and levers to protect from misuse
  • Fast Operation – reduce time to usage from around 30 sec to less than 5 Sec
    (in the case of frag grenades) to less than 2 sec (in the case of the magazines)
  • Ergonomic Design – Built for easy-to-use, single-hand, single-action operation
